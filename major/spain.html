Spain

Located in southwestern Europe, Spain is known for its warm Mediterranean beaches, beautiful historic builidngs such as La Sagrada Familia, and its exqusite cuisine!

 

    Food

Some of Spain's most famous dishes include:

  • Paella - Most certainly of one Spain's most iconic dish, you can enjoy this meal with either rabit and chicken or seafood. 

  • Lechazo - Made from cordero lechal, this lamb-based meal is particularly famous in the autonomous region of Castille and Leon

  • Gazpacho - This famous tomato-based Andalusian soup is served cold! A perfect meal for a hot summer day in the steets of Barcelona!

  • Pimentos de Padron - Originating in the municipality of Padron in northwestern Spain, the taste is usually mild. But be careful, some 
                                    can be quite hot!
