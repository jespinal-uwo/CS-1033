Top Spots

My favourite spots for others to discover on campus are:

 

Favourite spot to eat:

University Community Centre (UCC)



 

Located right in the middle of campus, the University Community Centre (UCC) contains a plethora of food options
which include restaurants such as:

  • Starbucks
  • Manchu Wok
  • Subways
  • Tim Hortons

The UCC cafeteria also holds numerous tables, making it a desirable destination to go and sit and eat with your friends.

Favourite spot to relax:

Middlesex College


The area that surrounds middlesex college is full of greenery and nature. Just outside the main entrance,
there are multiple benches that one can use to sit down and enjoy the scenery surrounding the beautiful
structure.


Favourite spot to study:

Allyn & Betty Taylor Library

The Allyn & Betty Taylor Library is perhaps one of the quietest libraries on campus. The library contains 3 floors, with
the lower 2 floors filled with individual cubicles designated to those who want to study in silence. The upper floor hosts
tables for those who want to study with friends. Because of this, the library offers you multiple options on how you
want to study.
